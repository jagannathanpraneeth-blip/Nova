# 🦅 Nova Wingman Mode

Nova is not just a productivity tool. It's now your **AI Wingman**.

## 💬 Social Intelligence

Nova can read your social media chats (WhatsApp, Instagram, Tinder, etc.) and give you real-time advice.

### How to use:
1.  **Open the Chat/Profile**: Make sure the conversation is visible on your screen.
2.  **Ask for Advice**:
    -   *"Nova, what should I reply to this?"*
    -   *"Nova, look at this profile. Any red flags?"*
    -   *"Nova, give me a witty opening line for this bio."*
3.  **Nova Analyzes**:
    -   Nova takes a screenshot (privately processed).
    -   It reads the context, tone, and vibes.
    -   It speaks/suggests the perfect response.

## 🧠 "Rizz" Engine
Nova's LLM prompt has been updated to include a "Social Expert" persona. It understands:
-   Humor and Wit
-   Flirting Dynamics
-   Conversation Flow
-   Red Flags

## 🔒 Privacy
-   Screenshots are processed by the LLM API and then discarded.
-   Nothing is stored permanently unless you ask Nova to save it.
